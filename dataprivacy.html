Data Protection Policy

At Mobile Boutique, we value your privacy and handle your personal data with care. This policy outlines how we collect and use your data, your rights, and our commitment to data security.

What We Cover

This policy applies to your use of our website. We have no control over external websites' data practices. Review their privacy policies before sharing data.

What is Personal Data?

Personal data includes any information that identifies you directly or indirectly, such as your name, contact details, or online identifiers.

Your Rights

You have rights under GDPR, including access to your data, correction of inaccuracies, and the right to be forgotten. Contact us for details.

What We Collect

We do not collect data unless you contact us. When you email us, we may collect your name, email, and provided information.

How We Use Your Data

We only use data you provide to respond to your email. We delete it within 30 days of issue resolution.

Data Storage

Your data is stored within the EEA to ensure GDPR compliance.

Data Security

We protect your data with an EV SSL Certificate.

Sharing Data

We do not share data except in legal compliance or court orders.

Accessing Your Data

You can request your data details through a Subject Access Request. Contact us for assistance.

Changes to Policy
